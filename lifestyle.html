Lifestyle

Healthy Lifestyle Basics

Sleep, water, steps, and daily habits that make fitness sustainable.

Basics

Sleep

7-9 hours, consistent timing, dark cool room.

Water

Sip through the day; start morning with water.

Movement

Aim 7-10k steps, stretch after long sitting.

Daily Habits

Sleep Early

Wind down, reduce screens before bed.

Limit Junk

Plan treats; keep weekday meals simple.

Screen Time

Block social media windows; focus mode.

Office + Gym + Study Balance

Plan

Weekly calendar for workouts and deep work.

Fuel

Pack meals; carry protein and water.

Recover

Sleep, steps, light mobility to stay fresh.

Stress Management

Breathing

2-3 slow minutes between tasks.

Journaling

Note wins, blockers, next steps.

Movement Breaks

Walk or stretch after long meetings.
