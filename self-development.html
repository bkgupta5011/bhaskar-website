My Personal Rules

  • Never skip two workouts in a row.
  • Protein in every main meal.
  • Steps before screen time in the evening.
  • Sleep before midnight on weekdays.
  • Track lifts weekly; progress in small jumps.
  • Weekend prep: groceries + batch cooking.
